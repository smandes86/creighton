About the msbia program

Today’s evolving business world needs leaders who can make critical technology decisions. Learn the skills you need to set the pace in this 33-credit online program. Courses include:

Survey of Business Intelligence and Analytics

Data Visual Analysis and Visualization

Database Management Systems

Information Systems Management

What you’ll learn

This AACSB-accredited program focuses on Big Data while preparing you to effectively work with business and technology teams. Learn how to:

  • Analyze financial and other key business data
  • Shape IT to drive business success
  • Recommend new systems, procedures or organizational changes
  • Grow your management and leadership skills
  • Understand business information processing systems
